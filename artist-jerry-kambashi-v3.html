They Don't Want Us Here

by Jerry Kambashi

I wanted to convey the danger of the unknown, and an accompanying sense of dread through this poster; hence, why I chose an astronaut in space. I also thought the astronaut looking directly at the viewer would be a striking visual.

Jerry is a designer and illustrator who loves all things minimalism. Playing volleyball and watching shows keep him sane throughout the week.
