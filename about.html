  • About IDEA Interprets

    (PLACEHOLDER TEXT FROM 2021)

    Growth—physical or mental, figurative or literal—is something we have all experienced. Whether it’s caring for a plant, overcoming obstacles, or advancing through life, we’ve been shaped into who we are today by growing.

    IDEA Interprets is an annual poster show and sale hosted by the students of Capilano’s IDEA School of Design. Each year features art and design centred around a common theme, and raises funds to further the students’ artistic pursuits.

    Join the class of ’21 as they present their own depictions of growth, and help support young local artists as they continue to grow.
